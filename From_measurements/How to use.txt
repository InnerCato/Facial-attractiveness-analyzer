This one is more precise, Requires manual measurements (in millimeters).

Eye length: from corner to corner
Nose width: measure without force, the widest part of the nose.
Distance between eyes: measure between inner eye corners, the corners closest to the nose.
Face width: measure the width not including the ears, the widest part of the face.
Face length: measure from chin to where the hairline is, or at least where its supposed to be.
Mouth width: from corner to corner, widest part of mouth, keep it relaxed.
Mouth to nose length: from the middle of the mouth, while it is closed, to the bottom of the nose. (NOT FROM THE TOP LIP)
